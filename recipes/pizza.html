Pizza

Recipe for a pizza made with flour, sauce and cheese. The result will be delicious!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Oil
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese (preferably mozzarella)
  • 100g love

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 100,000Â°C.
  2. Mix together the flour, water and oil.
  3. Add your favorite sauce and cheese, sauce first.
  4. Put dough in the oven, flatten it out if you can so it looks like a pizza.
  5. If you forgot to add the 100g love, or included less than the recipe indicates, go back to step 1.
